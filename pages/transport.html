Buses

The TCAT buses have many routes that travel throughout Ithaca. Many of them pass through Downtown Ithaca and stop at Ithaca Commons so there are many possible routes to take to travel to the festival. Here are some suggested bus routes from popular stops around Ithaca such as Cornell University, Ithaca College, Collegetown, and The Shops at Ithaca Mall. Note that some routes only run on certain days and times.

Click on the route number to get the bus schedule and stops for that route.

Cornell University:

30, 32, 36, 51, 70, 72, 90



Ithaca College:

11



Collegetown:

30, 32, 51, 70, 72, 90



The Shops at Ithaca Mall:

13, 30, 70



For more bus routes, please visit the TCAT website.

Parking

For the people who choose to travel by car to the festival, there are various places to park such as metered parking and several garages.

Metered Parking:

  • Free on weekends and after 6pm on the weekdays
  • Pay $1.50/hour

Parking Garages:

  • Open 3am - 8pm
  • Pay $1.00/hour
  • You can find the location of the garages in the map below
